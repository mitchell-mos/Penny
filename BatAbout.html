About Penny
Our story and mission.

Our Mission

Penny was developed to help college peers avoid early financial mistakes. We believe that securing your financial future shouldn't wait until after graduation. By blending powerful software with real-world financial coaching, we empower students to build lasting wealth today.

Who We Are

Founded in Summer 2026 by a senior wealth management student at Virginia Tech, Penny started as an independent financial coaching startup. It has since evolved into a comprehensive digital platform that provides budgeting tools, one-on-one advising, and specialized services tailored to young entrepreneurs.
